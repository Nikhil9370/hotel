About us

A foodie is a person who has an ardent or refined interest in food,
and who eats food not only out of hunger but also as a hobby.
The related terms "gastronome" and "gourmet" define roughly the same thing,
i.e. a person who enjoys food for pleasure; the connotation of "foodie" differs
slightly—a sort of everyman with a love for food culture and different foods.
Some, such as Paul Levy, say the foodie can still be a "foodist".
